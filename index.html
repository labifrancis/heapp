A good customer service...

A good customer service should wow the customer and provide easy access to 24/7 support. Also, a good customer support should offer great service that will make customers spread the word about the awesomeness of the service or product. Genuine care for customer satisfaction should be a priority for customer representatives. Finally, a customer representative should be humane with a sense of humor while relating with customers and anticipate their needs and solve them. These are the new philosophies about customer service I got after reading "Delivering Happiness" by Tony Hsieh (CEO, Zappos) and "Exceptional Service, Exceptional Profit”

The goal is to provide 24/7 customer support, mitigate customer disloyalty and by providing effortless experience. Anticipate customer needs and meet them. A good support rep should be able to dig out problems and find solutions. Andrew’s blog post - support career was an inspiration for me. It captured some of my thoughts especially the fact that support is about helping people and a lifetime of service. In support, you are the human connection to a company and its product. Your relationships with customers provide an opportunity to influence the company at a very foundational level. Support is about helping people by conveying technical information in a way they can relate to.
